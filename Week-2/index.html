about me

Hi. Welcome to my portfolio page. I am a first-year PhD student in the Social Sciences and Comparative Education program at UCLA, with a concentration in race and ethnic studies. My research interests lie at the intersection of education and racial equity. As a critical digital map maker I hope to elevate and uplift voices that often go unheard. I am looking forward to gaining technical skills that will complement the strength of qualitative methodologies.
